Built to support your 5-star food hygiene rating

The Food Standards Agency rates every UK food business on a 0–5 scale under the Food Hygiene Rating Scheme (FHRS). A top rating isn't just about being clean on the day — an environmental health officer will also assess your confidence in food safety management, and that confidence comes from documented records.

Veriqo gives you a complete, date-stamped paper trail. Every temperature check, cleaning log, and allergen declaration is stored against your account and exportable on demand. Whether you're a private chef registering for the first time or an established caterer preparing for a scheduled inspection, Veriqo keeps you ready.

One account. Two apps. Shared data.

Sign in once and both apps are connected. Clients you add in Carte appear in Veriqo's address book. Menus you build in either app sync across to the other. Job bookings show up in both so you always know what's coming.

Everything runs as a progressive web app (PWA) — install it on your iPhone or Android home screen and it works like a native app, even when signal is poor. Your records are encrypted and stored securely in the cloud, automatically backed up every time you save.

Veriqo is designed to meet the record-keeping requirements of the UK Food Safety Act 1990 and the Food Standards Agency's Safer Food Better Business guidance for caterers. All logs are date-stamped, stored with your account, and can be exported as a plain-text report.
